## Full description (≤4000 chars)
dotit is a personal guide that helps you understand yourself and make real decisions — in plain, human language.

Tell dotit when and where you were born, and it reads the unique pattern of that moment to reflect your strengths, your blind spots, the season you're in now, and where you're heading. No charts to decode, no jargon, no horoscopes — just a warm, grounded conversation that feels like it actually knows you.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Explore the parts of your life that matter — purpose and work, relationships, money, home and stability, health, and your deeper direction.
• Ask Now — bring a specific question that's sitting with you, and get a direct, considered answer for this exact moment.
• Listen — have any reading read aloud, at your own pace.
• Speak your language — dotit reads and listens in your language.

HOW IT FEELS
dotit talks to you the way a wise, honest friend would — specific, never vague; encouraging, never flattering. It leads with what's true for you, then opens a door toward your next step. It remembers the facts you share so each conversation builds on the last.

YOUR PRIVACY
Your birth details are used only to personalise your insight. dotit does not run ads, does not track you across apps, and does not sell your data. You can edit or permanently delete your data anytime from your Profile.

A GENTLE NOTE
dotit is for reflection and self-understanding. It is not medical, legal, financial, or psychological advice. For health concerns, please talk to a qualified professional.

Your dots were placed the moment you were born. dotit connects them.
